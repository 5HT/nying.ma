1 (ka)

     dus babs gter ston 'gro 'dul zil gnon blo gros sam/ bdud 'joms rdo rje'i skyes rabs gsol 'debs thos pa'i don ldan/ (skyes rabs)
    gsol 'debs gdung ba'i nges shes bskyed pa'i byin rlabs sprin phung/ (gsol 'debs)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: bla sgrub ye shes snying po'i brgyud 'debs byin rlabs char stsol/ (brgyud 'debs)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: bla sgrub ye shes snying po'i las byang nyi ma'i rgya can/ (bla sgrub)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: bla sgrub ye shes snying po'i 'dzab dgongs gnad kyi lde mig gsal byed chos sku'i mdzug tshugs/ ('dzab dgong)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: bla sgrub ye shes snying po'i bskang 'dus rin chen phreng ba/ (bskang ba)
  tshogs bsdus/
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: bla sgrub ye shes snying po'i don dbang yid bzhin nor bu'i skal ldan thar pa'i lam bzang rtsa dbang srog gi ti ka/ (rtsa dbang)
    yang gsang rang rig dril sgrub kyi brgyud pa'i gsol 'debs don gnyis lhun grub/ (brgyud 'debs)
     bla ma bde gshegs 'dus pa las/ yang gsang rang rig dril sgrub ye shes rol pa/
     bla ma bde gshegs 'dus pa las/ rig pa rtsal gyi dbang chog spyan gyis rgya can/ (dbang)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde: dag snang mkha' 'gro'i rgya can las: gu ru'i thugs sgrub yid bzhin dbang rgyal/
      gu ru'i thugs sgrub yid bzhin dbang rgyal las/ dbang chog pad+ma'i lcags kyu byin rlabs char 'bebs/ (dbang chog)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: bla ma'i thugs sgrub yid bzhin dbang gi rgyal po las/ brgyud pa'i gsol 'debs byin rlabs sprin phung/ (gsol 'debs)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: gu ru'i thugs sgrub yid bzhin dbang gi rgyal po'i las byang mi shigs rdo rje'i rgya can/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: bla ma'i thugs sgrub yid bzhin dbang gi rgyal po las/ byin 'bebs ye shes mchog rtsol/ (byin 'bebs)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: bla ma'i thugs sgrub yid bzhin dbang rgyal gyi bdud rtsi sman gyi mchod pa bdud rtsi zil mngar ye shes char 'bebs/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: gu ru'i thugs sgrub yid bzhin dbang rgyal gyi rtsa dbang srog gi ti ka ye shes rab 'bar/
    srog sgrub 'od gsal rdo rje las/ bla ma rgyal ba zhi khro'i las byang rtsa ba'i sgrub thabs rin chen thugs thig/ (las byang)
    srog sgrub 'od gsal rdo rje las: bla ma rgyal ba zhi khro'i bskang bshad rnam smin dag byed kyi zur rgyan thugs rje zla 'od/ (bskang bshag)
    srog sgrub 'od gsal rdo rje las: don dbang yid bzhin nor bu/
    srog sgrub 'od gsal rdo rje las: gshin po rjes 'dzin khrigs su bsdebs pa pad+ma 'od kyi zhing du bgrod pa'i bde chen lam gyi sgron me/
    srog sgrub 'od gsal rdo rje las: srung bzlog bsad pa'i don bsdus pa'i bsang bsur glud rabs lha'i gan+d+he/ (bsang bsur)
    rtsa gsum rgyal ba spyi sgrub las/ shwa mo srid gsum gsal byed/ (sho mo)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las/ dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba'i rtsa dbang srog gi ti ka skal ldan thar pa'i 'jug sgo/ (rtsa dbang)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod mi yi phreng ba'i rtsa dbang zur 'debs gsang ba'i lde mig (zur 'debs)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba'i skal ldan thar pa'i lam bzang zhal gdams man ngag bcu gsum ye shes spyan gyi rgya can/ (zhal gdams)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las/ dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba'i drag po'i sbyin sreg ye shes bskal pa'i me chen (sbyin sreg)

2 (kha)

    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las/ dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba yi: sgrub thabs me yi phreng ba dgra bgegs dpung 'joms/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las/ dbang drag gro lod: sgrub thab me yi phreng ba dgra bgegs dpung 'joms/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba: hUm sgrub ye shes thugs rje'i brgyud bskul byin rlabs char 'bebs/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba: dam can skyes bu chen po'i dgos bskul dgra tshar gcod/ (drag bskul)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba: mnan gtad chu srin gre rdzong dug ri lhun po/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: gro lod me yi phreng ba'i smad las bzlog pa spu gri rig gcod/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba bsreg blug gshin rje zhal bkab rngam byed 'khrug pa'i rba klong (gter gzhung)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba'i rgyal po glud rdzongs 'gong po skyal 'debs (rgyal mdos)
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba'i spring yig rdo rje pha lam/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: dbang drag gro lod me yi phreng ba'i lo glud 'gong po skyas 'debs/
   dbang drag gro lod me'i phreng ba'i tshogs bskang kha skong/
   dbang drag gro lod me'i phreng ba'i mchod bstod zur 'debs/
      ma rig rgyud bskul mnyam pa'i dgra bgegs/
    rtsa gsum dgongs pa kun 'dus las: brag skas ar tas gangs ri 'du spyod gsum pa'i gnas yig bskal ldan thar pa'i lam bzang/
     bla ma rtsa gsum bde gshegs spyi 'dus kyi las byang nyi ma'i dkyil 'khor chog 'grigs su bkod pa 'ja' tshon 'od kyi drwa ba/ (rtsa gsum)
    tshe sgrub chung zhags dmar po las: zla gsang tshe sgrub gsang ba zil gnon/ (tshe sgrub)
    tshe sgrub rlung zhag dmar po las: tshe yi dbang mchog rdo rje them skas/ (tshe dbang)
    tshe sgrub rlung zhag dmar po las: bla bslu cho ga don gnyis lhun grub/ (bla bslu)
    tshe sgrub rlung zhag dmar po las: dus min 'chi ba'i khrom bzlog 'chi bslu bsdus pa/ ('chi bslu)
   gsang ba ye shes dril sgrub las: nad brgya sman gcig spyi 'joms rdo rje pha lam yid bzhin dbang gi rgyal po/ (spyi 'joms)
    dkar gtor sngon 'gro bar chad kun sel/ (dkar gtor)
     bla ma nor bdag pad+ma rgyal po las/ bse rag rdzongs sa'i cho ga lan chags tshim byed/ (bse rag)
     bla ma nor bdag pad+ma rgyal po las/ las byang nor bu 'od phreng ba chog 'khrigs su bkod pa dpag bsam ljon shing dgos 'dod kun 'byung/ (las byang)
    bdud 'joms pa'i gter gsar nor bdag pad+ma rgyal po'i dpe'u ris/
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: g+yang chog 'dod rgu char 'bebs srid zhi dpal g+yang len pa ye shes thugs rje lcags kyu/ (g+yang len)
    gsol 'debs gdung ba'i nges shes byin brlabs sprin phung/ (gsol 'debs)
  dgongs gsang zab med ye shes klong mdzod las: rnam sras nor bdag sgrub pa dpag bsam shing gi 'bras bu/ (bum khyer)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: g+yang chos 'dod rgu char 'bebs kyi sngon rjes/ spyi ching gsang ba'i mdo 'dzin srog gi ti ka/ (gsang ching)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: g+yang chog 'dod rgu char 'bebs srid zhi dpal g+yang sgrub pa la dbang bskur 'dod rgu'i char 'bebs byin rlabs thugs rje lcags kyu/ (dbang chog)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: g+yang chog 'dod dgu'i char 'bebs srid zhi'i dpal g+yang sgrub pa gnod sbyin gter bum 'dod 'jo bum bzang/ (bum bzang)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: g+yang chog 'dod dgu'i char 'bebs srid zhi dpal g+yang sgrub pa la rgyas pa'i sbyin bsregs dpag bsam nyi ma'i yid bzhin dbang gis rgyal po/ (g+yang bsreg)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: bdud bzhi sel ba'i gto dpyad brgya bzhi don bsdu snying po/ (brgya bzhi)
    smon lam rnam mkhyen lam bzang byang chub shing rta/
     bar do smon lam 'khrul snang rang grol byang chen myur lam/
     bar do ngo sprod smon lam 'khrul snang rang grol ye shes thugs rjes rgyud bskul/

3 (ga)

   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: sngon 'gro don bsdus nyi ma'i snying po/
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las/ bcom ldan mgon po 'od dpag med kyi las byang pad+ma rA ga'i bklag chog tu bkod pa 'gro don sgo 'byed/ (las byang)

   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde dag snang mkha' 'gro rgya can las: mgon po 'od dpag med kyi sgrub thabs 'gro don sgo 'byed/ (gter gzhung)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: dag snang mkha' 'gro rgya can gyi 'od dpag med kyi dbang chog rin chen thugs thig (gter gzhung)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: dag snang mkha' 'gro'i rgya can las: mgon po 'od dpag med kyi rtsal dbang rin chen thugs thig (dbang chog)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde dag snang mkha' 'gro rgya can las: 'phags mchog thugs rje chen po'i sgrub thabs ma rig mun sel/ (gter gzhung)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: thugs rje chen po'i ma rig mun sel gyi las byang chog 'khrigs su bkod pa ngan song rang grol/ (las byang)
bkra shis tshig phreng/ (bkra shis)

   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: thugs rje chen po'i ma rig mun sel gyi dbang chog tshad med thugs rje lcags kyu/ (spyi gtsug dbang)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las/ thugs rje chen po'i ma rig mun sel gyi sgo nas gshin po rjes 'dzin thugs rje lcags kyu/ (byang chog)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: thugs rje chen po ma rig mun sel bdud rtsi sman gyi bsgrub pa myong ba rang grol/ (sman sgrub)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: thugs rje chen po'i ma rig mun sel gyi smin byed dbang gi cho ga zla ba rgyas pa'i 'dzum zer/ (lhan thabs)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las/ bde gshegs sman bla'i gsol 'debs gdon bgegs g+yul 'joms bar chad mun sel/
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: bde gshegs sman bla'i cho ga byin rlabs thugs rje char rgyun/ (sman chog)
sman srung gsol mchod mdor bsdus/
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: bde gshegs sman bla'i srung ma zhan blon gsol mchod 'dod dgu'i dpal gter/ (sman srung)
   klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las/ bde gshegs sman bla'i smin byed dbang gi cho ga bklag chog tu bkod pa'i bai Durya yi bum bzang/ (sman dbang)
dgongs gsang zag med ye shes klong mdzod las: 'jam dbyangs sgrub pa'i brgyud 'debs ye shes drwa ba/
dgongs gsang zad med ye shes 'od klong las: 'jam dpal sgrub pa ma rig mun sel ye shes sgron me/
bka' brgyad gsang ba yongs rdzogs las: bde gshegs rtsa ba'i srog bsgrubs/
bka' brgyad gsang ba yongs rdzogs las/ smad las gtor bzlog me yi 'khor lo dgra bgegs dpung 'joms/ (smad las)
bka' brgyad gsang ba yongs rdzogs las/ gter bsrung dpal ye shes kyi mgon po ma ning nag po rgyun gyi sgrub mchod tsi ta dmar byang phrin las 'dus pa/ (dpal mgon)
bka' brgyad gsang ba yongs rdzogs las: dbang bskur khro chu char 'bebs/
sems nyid ngo sprod skal ldan thar pa'i snying nor gsal ba'i me long rig pa ngo sprod/
sprul pa'i rig 'dzin dpal chen kun 'dus kyi cha lag gsol 'debs bar chad lam sel dang bsam pa lhun gnyis ldan gyi rnal 'byor yid dbang gi rgyal mo/

4 (nga)

klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdor sems sngon 'gro rin chen them skas/ (rdor sems sngon 'gro)

brgyud 'debs/
rdor sems zhi khro'i rten phreng mdzes las: dkar gtor gser skyem 'dod dgu rol mtsho/ (dkar gtor)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' na rag dwongs sprugs las las byang phrin las snying po'i me tog phreng mdzes/ (las byang)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdor sems zhi khro'i gar bteg phreng mdzes las 'dzab dgongs gnas kyi gzer bu tshad med thugs rje lcags kyu/ ('dzab dgong)
rdor sems na rag dongs sprugs las: 'chi med tshe yi sgrub pa srog gi ti ka/ (tshe sgrub)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las/ rdor sems na rag dongs sprugs kyi hUm sgrub sgyu lus sgra dbyangs byin rlabs char 'bebs/ (hUm sgrub)
rdor sems na rag dongs sprugs las: byin chen dbab pa ye shes mchog rtsol/ (byin dbab)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdor sems na rag dong sprugs kyi sngags srung ma mo bstod bskul gsol mchod glog gi phreng ba/ (sngags bsrung)
gter gsar gyi las byang kun la sbyar chog gi bkra shis dang 'dod dgu'i dpal gter/ (bkra shis)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las/ rdor sems zhi khro'i bsngo smon mkha' spyod myur lam/
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' dpal na rag dongs sprugs las gzhi lam 'bras gsum smon lam stobs po che/ (smon lam)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' na rag dwongs sprugs kyi zab gsang bka' gter srung ma sngags srung ma mo'i gsol mchod ci ta dmar 'byin/ (sngags srung)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdor sems zhi khro'i me tog phreng mdzes las bskang bshgags bag chags rang grol/ (bskang bshags)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde : dag snang mkha' 'gro rgya can las rdor sems sgrub thabs na rag dongs sprugs/ (las byang bsdus pa)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdor sems na rag dongs sprugs kyi bskang 'dus rin chen phreng ba dri med shel phreng/ (bskang ba)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' na rag dwongs sprugs phyag bshags na rag mtsho skem ye shes drwa ba ma rig mun sel/ (mtsho skem)
ye shes dkon mchog
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' na rag dongs sprugs kyi gnas 'dren 'phrad tshad rang grol bklag chog su sdebs pa tshad med thugs rje lcags kyu/ (gnas 'dren)
rdor sems zhi khro'i na rag dwongs sprugs las gnas 'dren tshad med thugs rje lcags kyu bsregs chog dug lnga rang grol/ (gnas 'dren)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde dag snang mkha' 'gro rgya can las: rdzogs chen brgyud pa'i gsol 'debs 'dod 'jo bum bzang/ (rdzogs chen)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' na rag dong sprugs rdzogs pa chen po'i thod rgal ye shes snang ba gser gyi sbu khu ye shes can gyi rgya can/ (rdzogs chen)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdor sems 'khrul 'khor dug 'joms rma bya chen mo/ (rtsa tshig)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' na rag dwong sprugs las rdzogs pa chen po'i ye shes klong grol gzhi lam 'bras gsum sang cad nyi ma snying po'i rdo rje mya ngan ma rig mun sel thugs rje lcags kyu/ (rdzogs chen)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' na rag dongs sprugs kyi rig pa rtsal thams cad dbang chog zab don gser gyi 'bru gcig/ (dbang chog)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' gsang sgrub ye shes spyan gyi rgya can 'khor 'das rang grol/ (gsang sgrub)
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde las: rdo rje sems dpa' yang gsang dril du sgrub pa'i nyi ma'i dkyil 'khor ye shes spyan gyi rgya can gsang ba ut+pal da sha dI ka/ (yang gsang)
bla ma rdo rje sems dpa'i sgom rim sprin bral zla ba rgyas pa'i mdzum zer/
klong gsal nam mkha'i chos sde dag snang mkha' 'gro'i rgya can las rdo rje sems dpa' zhi khro'i rtsa dbang ye shes bdud rtsi'i rgyun bzang/
