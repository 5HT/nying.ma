Kathok Rigdzin Tsewang Norbu

ཀཿ་ཐོག་རིག་འཛིན་ཚེ་དབང་ནོར་བུ

Volumes: 4
Versions: W23176

Kathog Rigdzin Tsewang Norbu (1698-1755) was an antiquary, statesman, and spiritual leader; two collections of his works survive. This so-called sung thorbu is the smallest but the regarded as the most accurate.
