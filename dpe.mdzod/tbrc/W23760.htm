Longchenpa Ngalso Korsum

ངལ་གསོ་སྐོར་གསུམ་སོགས

Volumes: 3
Versions: W23760

Trilogy of Natural Ease (ngalso korsum) and Trilogy of Natural Freedom (rangdrol korsum) are Dzogchen instructions by Longchen Rabjam Drime Ozer (1308-1364). Reprinted from the Adzom Drukpa Chogar woodblock edition, which is widely thought to be the best edition of Longchenpa's works.
