Kangyur Stog Palace

བཀའ་འགྱུར་༼སྟོག་པལཅེ་བྲིས་མ༽

Volumes: 109
Versions: W22083

A manuscript Kangyur from the Stog Palace near Leh in Ladakh. This was copied during the reign of Jamyang Namgyal from a Bhutanese manuscript reflecting the Kagyu tradition; the reprint is long out of print. The Kangyur includes a few 'duplicate' translations, at least one of which was excluded from the standard Kangyur redactions. It is thus very important for textual studies.
