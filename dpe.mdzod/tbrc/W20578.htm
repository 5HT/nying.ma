Rinchen Terdzo Chenmo

རིན་ཆེན་གཏེར་མཛོད་ཆེན་མོ

Volumes: 111
Versions: W20578

Treasury of Precious Revelations. Collection of rediscovered terma and commentatorial works. Comprises sadhanas, empowerments, and instructions of the Nyingma order gathered and structured by Jamgon Kongtrul Lodro Taye (1813-1899). Reproduction of the Todlung Tsurphu (stod lung mtshur phu) redaction. Supplemental texts from the Palpung (dpal spungs) blockprint redaction and manuscripts from other monasteries in the Kham region. Enlarged anthology, compiled at the order of Dilgo Khyentse Rinpoche.
