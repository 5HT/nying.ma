Guru Chowang Kagye

བཀའ་བརྒྱད་གསང་བ་ཡོངས་རྫོགས

Volumes: 4
Versions: W23819

Another collection of the the Kagye or Eight Pronouncements of the Mahayoga tradition is that revealed by Guru Chowang (1212-1270). The Tsamdrag manuscript set was reprinted by H.H. Dilgo Khyentse Rinpoche in order to restore the tradition.
