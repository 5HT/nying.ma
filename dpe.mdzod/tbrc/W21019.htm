Tsele Natsok Rangdrol

རྩེ་ལེ་སྣ་ཚོགས་རང་གྲོལ

Volumes: 8
Versions: W21019

Two manuscripts of the collected works of Tsele Natsog Rangdrol (b. 1608) have so far come to light. This eight volume collection is the largest and contains his most important writings on the relationship of Chagchen and Dzogchen.
