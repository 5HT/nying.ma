Chokgyur Lingpa

མཆོག་འགྱུར་གླིང་པའི་གཏེར་གསར

Volumes: 39
Versions: W22642

Chogyur Dechen Lingpa (1829-1870) was one of a Nyingma terton whose revelations spread widely throughout Tibet and remain today among the most important teachings of Tibet. He is regarded as one of the three pillars of the Rime or non-sectarian movement because of his outlook.
