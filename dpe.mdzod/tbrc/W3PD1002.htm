Jamyang Khyentse Wangpo

འཇམ་དབྱངས་མཁྱེན་བརྩེའི་དབང་པོའི་གསུང་འབུམ

One of the greatest minds of all times in the Tibetan tradition was Jamyang Kyentse Wangpo (1820-1892), another of the three pillars of the Rime tradition. His collected works reflect contain many of his notes and contain insights into his thinking on ecumenical approach.
