Dzongsar Khyentse Jamyang Chokyi Lodro

འཇམ་དབྱངས་མཁྱེན་བརྩེ་ཆོས་ཀྱི་བློ་གྲོས

Volumes: 10
Versions: W21814 W21813

One of the greatest teachers of the 20th century was Dzongsar Kyentse Jamyang Chokyi Lodro (1893-1959). His collected works were issued by his labrang in Gangtok for the benefit of his reembodiment, Dzongsar Khyentse Norbu. This is reproduced from this manuscript edition.
