Ratna Lingpa Tercho

རཏྣ་གླིང་པའི་གཏེར་ཆོས་༼དཔལ་ཡུལ་ལུགས༽

Volumes: 20
Versions: W23823-W23826 W21731-W21741 W3PD1003

The teachings of Ratna Lingpa (1403-1479), a terton of the southern Tibetan borderlands spread widely throughout eastern Tibet and were particularly practiced at Peyul Monastery. This collection was put together from rare manuscripts, all ume, from Namdrol Ling.
