Dorje Lingpa Tercho

རྡོ་རྗེ་གླིང་པའི་གཏེར་ཆོས

Volumes: 4
Versions: W23206

The first collection of teachings of the famed master of revelations, Dorje Lingpa (1346-1405) was published at the order of H.H. Dilgo Khyentse Rinpoche. It is this set that has been scanned here. Subsequently an almost complete collection of teachings of the Dorje Lingpa tradition was found by the late Lama Nuden Dorje (1930-1985) at the ancestral monastery of Tang Ogyan Choling. These were published in New Delhi and will be included in a subsequent hard drive.
